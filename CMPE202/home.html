How Mario Car works

Join

Apply online.
When you’re approved, we’ll send you a
member card in the mail.

Drive

Book a round trip car by the hour or day.
To unlock, tap your member card to the card reader on the windshield.

Return

When you're done, return the car to the
same location you picked it up from,
then lock up with your membership card.
Mario car live in your local neighborhood, and in cities, campuses and airports across the globe.
